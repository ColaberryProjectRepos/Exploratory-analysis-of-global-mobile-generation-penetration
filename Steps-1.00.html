Overview 1

Global and US telecom analyst view project

Data Source: GSMA

This project aims to do an exploratory analysis of the telecommunication subscriber and operator dynamics using GSMA data as a source.

Analysis highlights the mobile network market and technology dynamics providing

  • The ratio of mobile connections to the population in each country
  • Penetration of different mobile technology generations (1G, 2G, 3G, 4G,5G) in different countries over time
  • Change in USA mobile operator market share in terms of the number of connections and network coverage by pop over time
