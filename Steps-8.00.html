There are 2 data files from GSMA,

GSMA1 has many rows containing different metric with many attributes for different operator/country. As an example in the picture below it shows

  1. Total connection attribute of the metric Connections in the US level and also split across different operators.
  2. Female/Male/age group attributes of the metric population in the US level

GSMA2 has a row for each country with a number of connections and population of the country as of 2018. In addition also has the information of counties grouped under a region and subregion.
